Project: Public Attitudes Toward Artificial Intelligence
Goal: Analyze public opinions and emotions about AI across Reddit, Twitter, and Hacker News.
Technologies:
  - Python, Pandas, and Plotly for data analysis and visualization
  - Streamlit for interactive dashboard
  - Qwen Turbo API + LangGraph for conversational insights
  - Hugging Face sentiment and emotion models
  - Optional: BERTopic for topic modeling
Pipeline:
  1. Data acquisition from Reddit, Twitter (X), and Hacker News
  2. Cleaning and preprocessing
  3. Sentiment, emotion, and toxicity scoring
  4. Topic extraction
  5. Aggregation and visualization in dashboard
  6. Conversational Q&A with LangGraph + Qwen Turbo
